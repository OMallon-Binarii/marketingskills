## Problem Section

**Section title:** Traditional controls detect exposure. They do not eliminate it.

**Body copy:**
Healthcare and AI organizations generate massive volumes of unstructured data: clinical documents, model artifacts, research files, legal records, and operational reports. Most of it lives in cloud environments where complete files remain accessible if a provider, account, or credential is compromised.

For security and compliance leaders, that creates three persistent risks:
1. **Breach blast radius:** If attackers access storage, they can access full files.
2. **Availability fragility:** If one provider fails, teams lose access when they need it most.
3. **Audit pressure:** Proving who accessed what, when, and why is hard across fragmented systems.

**Transition line:** You cannot govern what your architecture still leaves fully exposable.

---

## Solution Section

**Section title:** Binarii Labs eliminates data exposure by design

**Body copy:**
Instead of monitoring threats around data, Binarii Labs secures the data itself.

Every protected file is automatically:
1. **Duplicated** for resilience
2. **Encrypted** for confidentiality
3. **Fragmented** so no location holds a complete asset
4. **Distributed** across at least three client-controlled storage locations

**Outcome statement:** If one cloud is breached or unavailable, attackers see only useless encrypted fragments while your authorized teams maintain continuity.

## Objections + FAQ

**Section title:** Common questions from healthcare and AI security teams

### 1) Does this replace our DLP and SIEM stack?
Binarii complements existing controls. DLP and SIEM help detect and investigate. Binarii reduces exposure at the data architecture layer.

### 2) How disruptive is deployment?
Deployment is designed for phased rollout. Teams can start with selected data domains and expand after validation.

### 3) Can we keep control over data location and sovereignty?
Yes. You choose and own the cloud locations used in your protection architecture.

### 4) What about performance and user experience?
Binarii is designed to protect data without introducing heavy user friction, manual tagging requirements, or day-to-day rule burden.

### 5) How does this support compliance audits?
Immutable Proof of Record provides tamper-resistant file activity history to support governance and audit workflows.
